# RepImprov
AI-Powered Workout Coaching Intelligence
We are building an AI-Powered Workout Coaching Intelligence by Posture/Form Analysis Plugin

Team Members: Hiroaki Okumura, Hutch Turner, Laxmi Balcha, Ethan Lee

Video Understanding AI Hackathon at Northeastern University

Built on FiftyOne x TwelveLabs Pegasus

Overview Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1CresPRs0JArARZPqaCJ2de8VckuZd_9sjOj1UekF0hc/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.utlwazc258n9

Dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/hasyimabdillah/workoutfitness-video

This plugin is designed to analyze and provide feedback on exercise form in the gym through pose estimation. It currently focuses on pushups and squats but can be applied to other exercises such as the benchpress or pull-ups. It can be used to help gym goers who don't have personal trainers or a perfect understanding of form. This plugin can allow them to analyze their own form and receive free feedback in a timely manner. It could also be used in research so researchers can ensure subjects are maintaining a consistent form quality during repetitions, as form variation could be a variable in muscle production, stretch, injury, or anything else researchers may be analyzing.
